Hi, I'm Heather! 

I investigate the mechanisms through which genetic diversity is created, maintained, lost, and restored-- primarily in large carnivores. I aim to understand interactions between ecology and evolution and inform conservation strategies that maintain biodiversity while mitigating human–wildlife conflict.

I maintain my publications on [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com) and code on [GitHub](https://github.com/hclendenin).
